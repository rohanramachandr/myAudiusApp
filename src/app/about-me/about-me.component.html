Why I Want To Join The Gallify Team

Although I am not particularly good at drawing or painting, I always think of myself as an artistic person because I enjoy the process of creating. Whether it be artistically crafting the aesthetics of the front end of an application or writing a song, I find that the process of creating often brings out the most authentic version of myself and others.

Unfortunately, it is impossible to not notice that most people who choose a career in creating any form of art often struggle to profit from their works. It is even more disheartening to know that many large corporations profit from the works of artists while the artists themselves do not.

As a young software developer, this issue inspires me more than any other and is what drew my interest towards blockchain technology. I read about up and coming blockchain driven startups like Audius, which is creating a fully decentralized music-streaming platform made for musical artists in which they will be able to set their own price per stream of their songs. From then and on, I made it my goal to become a blockchain developer and work for this type of company, so that I could play a role in creating a platform that would help other creators continue creating.
