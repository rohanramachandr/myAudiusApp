Why I Want To Join The Twilio Team

As I start my career as a new graduate software engineer, I have established a principle that I will only work for companies that have three specific qualities. The first quality being that the company I work for must have an overall positive impact on the world. The second quality being that I must find the company’s products and services cool and exciting. The last quality the company must have is the willingness to push me outside of my comfort zone.

Twilio stood out to me because I feel it surpasses all three of these requirements. Twilio’s ability to create tools that enable customer engagement in a way that was never easily accessible before is creating a positive impact through empowering businesses and developers across the globe. Furthermore, using Twilio SDKs and APIs as a developer myself excites me because of all the side project possibilities they enable. Finally, I am a firm believer that work and life start to get dull when I am not continuously improving myself. For this reason, I am applying to Twilio because I know that the projects involved with working at Twilio are challenging and will help me grow as a developer.
